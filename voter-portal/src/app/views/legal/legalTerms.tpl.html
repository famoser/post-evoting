Penal provisions concerning the use of the voting portal

Anyone in particular may be punished with imprisonment or a fine under Articles 279 to 283 of the Criminal Code:
  • uses a false identity or the identity of another voter;
  • votes more than once in a voting template;
  • alienates, misappropriates or adds ballot papers;
  • attempts to destroy, destroy, modify or alter ballot papers, registers or other materials used to determine the results of votes, in whole or in part.

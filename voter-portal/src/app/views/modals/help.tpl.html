Help

How do I start?

To start the voting process, please enter the Start Voting Key. The Start Voting Key is on the printed Voting Card that you have received by mail.

To start the voting process, please enter the Start Voting Key as well as your year of birth. The Start Voting Key is on the printed Voting Card that you have received by mail.

To start the voting process, please enter the Start Voting Key as well as your date of birth. The Start Voting Key is on the printed Voting Card that you have received by mail.

What is the Voting Card?

Your Voting Card contains the needed keys and codes for the voting process. You will receive it via postal mail.

In the Voting Card you will find:

  • Start Voting Key: you will have to introduce this key to get into the voting platform.
  • Choice Codes: you will have to verify that the displayed codes match the ones you have in your Voting Card.
  • Ballot Casting Key: you will have to introduce this key to validate your options and cast the vote.
  • Vote Cast Code: you will have to verify this code is the same as the one you have on your Voting Card.
What is the Start Voting Key?

The Start Voting Key (identified with a TRIANGLE), is a set of numbers or a combination of letters and numbers provided in your Voting Card via postal mail. You will have to type them in the first screen of the portal to identify yourself and to start the voting process. Note: If you enter a wrong key or leave spaces in blank the system will inform you with an error message.

How to make a choice?

For any question provided, you can either choose YES, NO or leave it blank if you wish to cast a blank vote.

You can select your options by clicking the checkboxes in the list. If you wish to change your choices deselect the checkbox and click in the final option box.

Can I change my selection?

Once you are in the review and seal screen, you can change your options by clicking on the link “change” If your selection is final, click on the “Seal” button to seal the ballot. You will not be able to change it electronically after this point. This process may take a few seconds.

What are the Choice Codes?

The Choice Codes are also provided in your Voting Card via postal mail and are identified with a DIAMOND.

The Choice Codes are an array of numbers that will be displayed onscreen together with the list of questions.

For each question, you should check that the displayed code is the one that identifies your selection. This code confirmation is compulsory for security purposes.

What is the Ballot Casting Key?

The Ballot Casting Key (identified with a PENTAGON) is a key to validate your options and cast the vote.

Once the displayed codes match the ‘Choice Codes’ you should type the ‘Ballot Casting Key’ (provided in the Voting card by traditional mail) to confirm your vote. This action will effectively cast your vote.

What is the Vote Cast Code?

You will have to verify that the displayed code matches the ‘Vote Cast Code’ identified with a STAR in your Voting Card. If they both match then the voting process has been completed successfully.

What if codes do not match?

You should contact the Election support team.

What is the difference between seal and cast?

“Sealing” a vote means to include your choices into a virtual envelop. To complete the voting process you will need to cast it. If you leave the process at this stage you will be able to return later on but you will not be able to change your options. To do so you will need to vote in paper through the traditional way. “Casting” a vote means to include this envelope with your choices into a ballot, making your vote effective. However, whenever a vote is cast, it is considered a final submission and therefore the voter is not able to vote again in the same election.

Can I leave or cancel my voting anytime?

You are able to leave the voting process at any time before casting your vote. Remember that in order to cast your vote and to complete the voting process you must enter the Ballot Casting Key, sealing your options is not enough. If you leave the process without sealing your vote your selection will be lost. If you leave the process after sealing your vote your selection will be saved. If you have sealed your options but you have not verified them by entering the Ballot Casting Key, your vote will not be cast. You will have to return to the platform to confirm your vote with the Ballot Casting Key to make it effective.

How can I use write-ins?

Click on "Choose" or "Change". At the bottom, you will find the possibility to select a write-in option. By selecting this option, you can enter your choice in a write-in field.

You just need to type the name and first name of the desired candidate into the field provided to this aim. If possible, please provide additional information (year of birth or address) to clearly identify the candidate.
